Your Personal, Portable, Focus-Boosting Study Hub

What is FocusPod?

FocusPod is a foldable, portable student workspace system designed to help students block distractions, stay organized, and improve classroom focus — especially for those with attention challenges like ADHD.

📱 Integrated iPad Stand

Secure middle slot to hold your tablet at the perfect angle for work or reading.

📝 Removable Whiteboards

Write, doodle, solve problems, or brainstorm ideas right next to your workspace. Wipe clean and reuse instantly.

📡 Student-Teacher Communication

Raise your digital hand, receive encouragement emojis, or discreetly ask for help without disrupting the class.

💼 Foldable Briefcase Design

Lightweight and portable — folds into a sleek carrying case to take from class to class.

Why FocusPod?

  • Helps students with ADHD block out distractions.
  • Reduces digital overwhelm while keeping necessary tools nearby.
  • Encourages healthy focus habits in a physical, interactive workspace.
  • Makes classrooms more flexible, organized, and personal.
